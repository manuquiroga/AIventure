FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

¿What is AIVENTURE?

- AIVENTURE is an interactive story game, "choose your own adventure" style mixed with a spice of the roleplay genre .

¿How to play?

- In order to play, you must go to the character creation section and click the empty card where the text "new" appears, then create your character.

- Once the character is created press the play button located on the character card and set up your story to your liking

¿What if I don't have any actions left?

- If you have no more actions left, then you can get more at the add actions section.

¿How can I have the best user experience with AIventure's AI?

- In order to have the best user experience possible, all inputs that implie an action must start with your character's name followed by the action you want to perform.

- Example: Tony shouts to his comrades to back off.

¿What else can I do with AIventure's AI?

- Keep in mind that our model allows you to explore and do unexpected things, you can create events and characters, but also have conversations with said characters.

- Example: Tony encounters a Giant Spider with venom dripping from it's fangs.

¿How long are AIventure stories?

- The lenght of the story depends on two factors, first on the number of actions you have left. Second, on how long you can go.

- Sometimes, if the context gives foot to the AI to end the story, it will but otherwise you can keep going forever and end the story yourself with the "End" button.
